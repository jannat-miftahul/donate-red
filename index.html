Need More Help?

Can't find what you're looking for? Our support team is here to help you with any questions.

What are the requirements for donating blood?

To donate blood, you must be at least 18 years old, weigh at least 110 pounds, and be in good health. You must also not have donated blood in the last 56 days. Additional health screenings will be conducted at the donation center.

How long does the blood donation process take?

The blood donation process typically takes about 30 minutes to an hour, including registration, medical screening, donation, and refreshments. The actual blood collection takes only 8-10 minutes.

Is it safe to donate blood?

Yes, donating blood is completely safe. The equipment used is sterile and disposable, and the donation process is closely monitored by trained medical professionals. All safety protocols are strictly followed.

What should I do before donating?

Before donating, make sure to eat a healthy meal, drink plenty of water, and get a good night's sleep. Avoid alcohol and caffeine 24 hours before donation. Bring a valid ID and any required documents.
